Adil’s Journey: From Dreams to Reality

Adil, a 20-year-old student from Bangladesh, dreamed big—bigger than most. With aspirations of studying at Harvard University and becoming an ML engineer, his life was a canvas waiting for bold strokes of success.

The Beginning

Adil woke up every day at 6 AM, filled with a sense of purpose. His mornings were not about rushing to keep up with the world but about setting the pace for his ambitions. A 15-hour winter routine became his mantra, where five hours were dedicated solely to Machine Learning (ML), 1.5 hours to academic studies, and another 1.5 hours to book reading. English learning and LeetCode challenges also found their rightful place in his daily schedule.

Adil’s passion for coding had no bounds. He started his journey by creating simple yet elegant websites. His GitHub became a treasure trove of projects like his Tic-Tac-Toe game and the ‘50 Days of Machine Learning’ series. Each upload reflected his dedication and growth, from Day 1’s baby steps to Day 53’s advanced concepts. The repository didn’t just mark progress; it told a story of perseverance.

Building Dreams

One day, Adil decided to create a mobile app. With a vision for simplicity and style, he designed a Q&A quiz app, focusing on Python and Java. The app wasn’t just functional; it was a masterpiece of iOS-like design with soothing sky-pink hues. He ensured that users could easily navigate through Python and Java questions, each in separate files. The app became a hit among his peers, inspiring him to explore new horizons.

Enter ‘Sonargaon Glow Store’

Adil’s entrepreneurial spirit wasn’t confined to coding. He launched ‘Sonargaon Glow Store,’ an online hub for cosmetics, skincare, and beauty essentials. The store’s motto—“Glow Confidently”—resonated with customers, making it a trusted name in beauty products. Adil’s knack for blending business with creativity shone as brightly as the products he curated.

AdilTechInsights

Not one to rest on his laurels, Adil launched his blog, ‘AdilTechInsights.’ The blog became a platform to share his thoughts on Machine Learning, AI, and other tech marvels. Every post was a deep dive into the cutting-edge world of technology, written with a simplicity that even a class 10 student could grasp. It was this ability to make complex topics accessible that earned Adil a loyal readership.

Triumphs and Challenges

Adil’s path was not without hurdles. Learning Machine Learning, Generative AI, and DSA from scratch tested his resilience. But with a dedicated coding tutor and a roadmap tailored to his needs, he steadily progressed from beginner to advanced levels. His efforts bore fruit when he cracked over 100 LeetCode problems before the end of 2024, sharing his triumphs on Twitter with his growing audience.

The Visionary

Adil’s vision extended beyond personal achievements. He aimed to use AI to help companies grow, imagining a future where his innovations could transform businesses. His entrepreneurial streak and technical prowess made him a force to reckon with in the tech world.

The Future Beckons

As 2025 began, Adil embarked on building iOS and Android apps for note-taking and simple Q&A interfaces. Each project reflected his growth, creativity, and relentless pursuit of excellence. Adil’s journey was far from over; it was just beginning. With Harvard on the horizon and his aspirations as his compass, Adil was poised to leave an indelible mark on the world.
